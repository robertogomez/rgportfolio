About me

I'm a San Diego-based aspiring software developer. I studied Computer Engineering at San Diego State University from 2009-2012. My greatest strengths lie in developing applications with the C family of languages and programming devices such as microcontrollers and FPGAs. I am currently looking for new challenges to apply my knowledge.


I am fascinated by computers and digital technology. Some of the current technologies that interest me and wish to explore include app development for mobile devices, web apps, and web development.


I enjoy tinkering with computers in areas such as open source and Linux, custom pc building, and overclocking hardware.


Some of my other interests include video games, biking, and tennis.
