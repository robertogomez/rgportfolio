About me

I'm a San Diego-based aspiring software developer. I studied Computer Engineering at San Diego State University from 2009-2012. My greatest strengths lie in developing applications with the C family of languages and programming devices such as microcontrollers and FPGAs. I am currently looking for new challenges to apply my knowledge.

I am fascinated by computers and digital technology. Some of the current advances that I would like to explore include app development for mobile devices and web development.

I enjoy tinkering with Linux, building custom PCs, and overclocking hardware.

Some of my other interests include video games, biking, and poker.
