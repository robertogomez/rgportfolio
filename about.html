About me

I'm a senior student studying Computer Engineering at San Diego State University. I will be graduating in the Fall of 2014. My greatest strengths lie in developing applications with the C family of languages and programming devices such as microcontrollers and FPGAs. I am currently looking for new challenges to apply my knowledge.

I am fascinated by computers and digital technology. Some of the latest advances that I'm currently exploring include web development and mobile app development.

I enjoy tinkering with Linux, building custom PCs, and overclocking hardware.

Some of my other interests include video games, biking, and poker.

About my portfolio

Working on this site has been a fun learning experience. I created it myself, without using any portfolio template or WYSIWYG editor, by hand coding the HTML and CSS. I chose to make it simple and accessible by focusing on its layout, typography, and colors. During its development I also learned about graphics editing and version control systems.
