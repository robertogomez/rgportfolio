About me

In Dec. 2014 I graduated from San Diego State University with a B.S. in Computer Engineering. My greatest strengths lie in developing applications with the C family of languages and programming devices such as microcontrollers and FPGAs. I am currently looking for new opportunities to apply my knowledge.

I enjoy tinkering with Linux and building custom PCs.

Some of my other interests include video games, biking, and poker.

About my portfolio

Working on this site has been a fun and engaging experience. I wrote all the HTML and CSS myself, without using any templating software. I chose to make it simple and accessible by focusing on its layout, typography, and colors. I utilized the Bootstrap framework to provide responsive functionality, and added icon fonts from Font Awesome for the footer links. During its development I also learned about graphics editing and version control systems. I'm proud of the results and look forward to improving my portfolio's design.
